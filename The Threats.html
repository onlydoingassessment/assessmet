THE THREATS



PHISHING


Phishing is the act of sending fraudulent messages to a target via several methods such as email and text messages. Phishing using SMS is also called Smishing. Both Phishing and Smishing involve waiting for their target to compromise their system due to the message, before striking, and "catching" the fish by activating malware or getting personal and important information.



BOTNETS


Bot nets are a collection of bots, brought under the same threat actor. Bots are simply regular devices infected witha malware that allows a threat actor to remotely command or control them. Botnets can be used to perform further attacks such as malware spreading and DDoS attacks where a flood of requests are send to overwhelm as system.



EVIL TWINS


Evil Twins are compromised networks posing to be a real network in an attempt to get targets to connect to them. Once a target has connected, all data sent over that network would likely be compromised and stolen by the hacker.



MALWARE


Malware is a general term used for any malicious code that brings harm to a victim. Most forms of attacks aim to bring malware into the victims device. Malware comes in different forms such as trojans, worms, rootkits and ransomeware, all with different effects..
