THE IMPORTANCE OF CYBERSECURITY



In a world where the internet shifts faster than tides, having strong cybersecurity is essential. The safety of entire systems and those who rely on it is linked to the cybersecurity of it, placing great importance on knowing the threats and their extent, as well as how to deal or reduce them.

CYBERSECURITY PRACTICES



The broadness of cybersecurity makes it difficult to protect against everything, but there are some important steps that should taken to at least reduce the threat. Strong passwords are one of the most commonly stated methods, for a good reason. Passwords are like keys, anyone with them can gain access. Having a weak password compromises all systems related to them, leading to serious consequences. Up to date systems is another one. Exploits are found all the time, and older systems will almost certainly have an exploit, making it excessively easy for a threat actor to gain access to them.
