PHISHING



An estimated 3.4 billion phishing scams a day, making it one of the most prominent scams in the world.

BOTNETS



There are thousands of botnets in the world, with over 25% of the worlds internet traffic being malicious bots.


Botnets have caused over 150,000 DDoS attacks a year, resulting in billions of damage in monetary loss.

It is impossible to predict the amount of evil twin networks, but it can be assumed that there millions of evil twin networks.


The damage evil twin networks do is a difficult amount to predict, as it can be hard to identify one.
