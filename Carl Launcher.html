WHAT IS THIS SITE ABOUT?



This site is a compilation about data in relation to cyber security, created for a Computing Elective Project. It contains data about several cyber threats that can be seen in the internet, while using an analogy that likens the internet to a sea to create an immersive and engaging experience. Threats are displyed in the exhibition as well as additional data such as the frequency of the threat, the total monetary loss from the threat and what can be done about it.

HOW DOES IT WORK?



The general majority of the information in this sight can be found within the exibition. To enter the exibition, simply click on the button above START EXIBITION. You will then be taken to a showcase of different threats where you can read a quick overview of it or click SHOW DATA, which then will show data about the threat. You can then click on the buttons on the left or right to go to the next threat.
